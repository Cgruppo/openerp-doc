*****************
管理体系
*****************

.. i18n:  *A management system is the framework of processes and procedures used to ensure that an 
.. i18n:  organization can fulfill all tasks required to achieve its objectives.*
..

 *管理体系是过程和程序的框架，用来保证组织机构可以有效地完成所有需要的工作，从而达到目标*

.. i18n: The objective of management system is to provide all the required applications to manage your
.. i18n: manuals, procedures, environmental aspects, work instructions, audits, nonconformities, 
.. i18n: improvement opportunities, immediate, corrective and preventive actions.
..

The objective of management system is to provide all the required applications to manage your
manuals, procedures, environmental aspects, work instructions, audits, nonconformities, 
improvement opportunities, immediate, corrective and preventive actions.

.. i18n: For this chapter you should start with a fresh database that includes demo data,
.. i18n: with :mod:`mgmtsystem_quality`, or :mod:`mgmtsystem_environment` and their dependencies installed.
..

For this chapter you should start with a fresh database that includes demo data,
with :mod:`mgmtsystem_quality`, or :mod:`mgmtsystem_environment` and their dependencies installed.
