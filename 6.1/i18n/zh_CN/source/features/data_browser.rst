Cube Browser
============

.. i18n: The Business Intelligence cube browser helps end-users easily navigate
.. i18n: through the information and statistics of the company and design customised
.. i18n: reports with a few drag and drop operations. You can consolidate, drill up, drill down, slice
.. i18n: and filter data from a very user-friendly report interface.
..

The Business Intelligence cube browser helps end-users easily navigate
through the information and statistics of the company and design customised
reports with a few drag and drop operations. You can consolidate, drill up, drill down, slice
and filter data from a very user-friendly report interface.

.. i18n: The power of Business Intelligence becomes available to all users of the
.. i18n: system to extract the exact information needed. It is also
.. i18n: able to analyse other databases, allowing you to consolidate data in a
.. i18n: single user interface.
..

The power of Business Intelligence becomes available to all users of the
system to extract the exact information needed. It is also
able to analyse other databases, allowing you to consolidate data in a
single user interface.
