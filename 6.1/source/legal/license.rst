OpenERP Server, Addons, GTK Client
++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++

The OpenERP server, the official OpenERP modules and the OpenERP desktop client (GTK) are
released under:

* `GPLv3 <http://www.gnu.org/licenses/gpl.html>`_ in version 5.0
* `AGPLv3 <http://www.gnu.org/licenses/agpl.html>`_ as of version 6.0

Some contributions and embedded libraries are released under other specific
`GPL-compatible licenses <http://www.dwheeler.com/essays/floss-license-slide.html>`_,
in which case the licensing terms are specified in the corresponding source code files
(and on the author's or project website).
